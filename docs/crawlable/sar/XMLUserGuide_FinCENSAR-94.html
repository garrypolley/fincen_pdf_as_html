PartyAccountAssociation

1
Description:
Party account association (header). This is the container for creating the association of the account to
the Financial institution where the account is held, as well as indicating whether or not the account is
closed.
The following sub-element is associated with this element:
1.
Subject: Financial institution TIN and account number(s) affected that are related to the
subject (if any) (FinCEN SAR Part I, Item 27e)
